About me

I am Ignacio R. Yurag, from Medellin, Cebu. We are a total of nine (9) siblings and I graduated my Junior and Senior High School in Medellin National High School (MNHS). Luckily passed the exam in Passerelles Numeriques (PN) as they gave opppurtunities to those students who cannot proceed to college. I had a doubt that time if I will continue my studies because my plan is to find a job so that can help sustaining the needs of my family. But, suddenly my mind changed when the arrival of those selected scholars for that said date. Don't know what came to my mind and accidentally decide to proceed. Is it God's plan? But all I know everything has a reason and purpose of what I am now as a PN-scholar. So now, I dont have time to give up but a willingness to continue my goals in life. For this two years and half, I will make sure to be a better person in the future.
